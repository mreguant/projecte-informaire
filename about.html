Our Team

We are a team of parents and social scientists who have become a bit frustrated by the lack of detailed local pollution data. With this initiative, we are hoping to fill this gap while engaging with the education community to re-think what we can do to address this major challenge.

Mar Reguant

Mar Reguant is an Associate Professor of Economics at Northwestern University and research associate at the Barcelona GSE. Her research research concerns the energy transition and the study of electricity and pollution markets.

Aina Gallego

Aina Gallego is an Associate Professor of Political Science at the University of Barcelona and a research associate at the Barcelona Institute for Political Economy and Governance. She studies political behavior with a focus on political inequality, technology, and gender.

Martí Mestieri

Martí Mestieri is a senior researcher at the Federal Reserve Bank of Chicago and visiting professor at the Barcelona GSE and UPF. His research focuses on the study of cross-country and household inequality.
