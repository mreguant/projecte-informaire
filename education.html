Teaching materials

An important part of the InformAire Project is to get into the classrooms and teach about pollution and climate science. This space serves as a way to share educational content related to the sensors initiative.

Education is Important

We need to raise awareness about how our daily activities can impact pollution and the environment more broadly. Knowing about pollution is also a great way to learn about how to protect ourselves. When a school sensor indicates high levels of pollution, teachers and students can learn to re-think the outdoor activities and act accordingly. At a basic level, students can learn about the sources of pollution, how they interact with the air, their consequences, etc. At a more advanced level, students can learn about how to use the hourly pollution data created by the sensor to plot the patterns of pollution while learning how to code.

Note: There are only few educational materials at this point. We plan to greatly expand them as the project advances.
